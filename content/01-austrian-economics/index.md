## 🎯 What is Sound Money?

**Sound Money = Money yang punya 3 karakteristik:**

### 1. **Scarce** (Langka)
- ❌ **Fiat:** Infinite supply - central banks print sesuai keinginan
- ✅ **Bitcoin:** 21,000,000 BTC max - **HARDCODED, can't change**

### 2. **Durable** (Tahan lama)
- ❌ **Paper money:** Degradasi fisik, bisa robek, terbakar
- ✅ **Bitcoin:** Digital, immutable, permanent di blockchain

### 3. **Divisible** (Bisa dibagi kecil)
- ❌ **Gold:** Susah divide ke unit kecil (gram, ons)
- ✅ **Bitcoin:** Divisible sampai 8 decimal places (1 satoshi = 0.00000001 BTC)

## ⏰ Time Preference: The Most Important Concept

**Time Preference = Preferensi consume NOW vs SAVE for LATER**

### **High Time Preference** (Fiat Mindset)
```
Money loses value over time (inflation 5-10% per year)
↓
Rational behavior = SPEND NOW
↓
No incentive to save
↓
No capital accumulation
↓
Poverty cycle
```

**Example:**
- 2020: $100,000 saved
- 2024: Worth only ~$80,000 (purchasing power)
- **Lost 20% doing nothing!**

---

### **Low Time Preference** (Sound Money Mindset)
```
Money GAINS value over time (deflationary / stable)
↓
Rational behavior = SAVE for FUTURE
↓
Incentive to defer gratification
↓
Capital accumulation
↓
Wealth building
```

**Example Bitcoin:**
- 2020: 1 BTC = $10,000
- 2024: 1 BTC = $60,000
- **Gained 6x by HODLing!**

## 📉 Fiat Money Problem: The Cantillon Effect

**Cantillon Effect = Those closest to money printer benefit most**

### **How Fiat System Works:**

1. **Central Bank prints money** ($2 trillion COVID stimulus)
2. **Banks get it FIRST** → buy assets (stocks, real estate)
3. **Asset prices inflate** (houses, stocks go up)
4. **Rich get richer** (own assets)
5. **Money trickles down** to regular people (months/years later)
6. **Regular people face inflation** (grocery, rent up)
7. **Poor get poorer** (wages don't keep up)

**Data:**
- **2020-2024:** US printed ~$5 trillion
- **Stocks:** S&P 500 up 80%
- **Housing:** Median home up 40%
- **Wages:** Up only 15%
- **Groceries:** Up 25%

**Who wins?** Asset holders (rich)
**Who loses?** Wage earners (poor & middle class)

## 💡 Key Austrian Economics Principles

### 1. **Subjective Theory of Value**
- Value ditentukan individual, bukan intrinsic
- Bitcoin valuable karena people CHOOSE to value it
- "Bitcoin has no intrinsic value" = WRONG QUESTION
  - Neither does gold, fiat, or anything else
  - Value = what people willing to exchange for it

### 2. **Marginal Utility**
- Additional unit = less valuable
- Example: 1st pizza = very valuable, 10th pizza = less valuable
- Bitcoin: As supply approaches 21M cap → **each BTC more scarce → more valuable**

### 3. **Regression Theorem**
- Money emerges from commodity (has use case first)
- Gold → used in jewelry/electronics → became money
- Bitcoin → used by cypherpunks → became digital cash → store of value
